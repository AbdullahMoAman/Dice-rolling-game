📌 Overview

This is a browser-based dice game for two players.
The objective is to be the first player to reach 50 points.

🎮 How to Play

The game supports 2 players.

Players take turns rolling the dice.

On your turn:

Click Roll Dice to roll.

The rolled number is added to your current score.

If you roll a 1, you lose your current score, and your turn ends.

Click Hold to save your score to the total.

The first player to reach 50 points wins.
